Extending:

I have inserted comments into the code so you can easily find the section you would like to delete or duplicate. Just copy or delete the beginning of the first comment to the end of the second comment (exactly) to duplicate or delete the entire section.

Images:

The easiest way to enter an image will be to either just replace the placeholder images here by right click>image properties>*insert your new image link*, or to copy the image TR section for the column setup you are in via the 'extending' method above.

Currently, images are designed to be 600px wide (full width) in the one column setup, 280px wide in the two column setup, and 180px wide in the 3 column setup. All heights are specified to be 'auto' in the stylesheet. Please do not specify height when entering an image.
